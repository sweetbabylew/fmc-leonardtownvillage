Where we are

The community sits in a settled, walkable part of Leonardtown, so daily needs stay close. Two of the things residents value most are within walking distance: the College of Southern Maryland's Leonardtown campus and St. Mary's Hospital — a real convenience for students and for anyone who works in health care.

Getting around

The location keeps the region within easy reach: Washington, D.C., and King George, Virginia, are each about an hour away, and Solomons Island, with its waterfront shops and marinas, is a short drive out for a day on the water.
